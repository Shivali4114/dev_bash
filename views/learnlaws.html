Learn Child Laws

Understand your rights and responsibilities under child protection laws.

Child Rights

Children have the right to education, protection, and health care, among others.

Legal Age

The age of majority is typically 18, but this varies by jurisdiction. At this age, individuals can make legal decisions.

Legal Protection

Children are legally protected from abuse and exploitation. There are laws to ensure their safety at home, school, and society.

Quick Facts

Learn important laws related to children.

Child Marriage Laws

Child marriage is prohibited in many countries to protect young individuals from early marriages.

Education Rights

Every child has the right to free education until a certain age as prescribed by local laws.

Child Labor Laws

Child labor is illegal, and children have the right to be free from exploitation at work.
